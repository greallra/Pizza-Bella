ABOUT

Pizza Bella

Our Story

Here at Pizza Bella, we serve real Romana style Pizza, using the highest quality 00 Flour, San Marzano tomatoes, Fiore Di Latte Mozzarella from Campania and the finest toppings.

We source our products locally where possible and also from various regions of Italy. Freshness and quality is important to our team who make all of our food daily in our kitchen.

Our Mission

We believe, with our home made ingredients, and our methodologies and best practises, we have the edge over our competition.

In addition to our expertise, we export our produce from various regions of Italy. It is important to use that we ensure high quality food to our customers.
